Welcome to Hockessin, Delaware

About Hockessin

Hockessin is a charming community located in New Castle County, Delaware. Known for its rich history, picturesque landscapes, and a strong sense of community, Hockessin offers a blend of rural and suburban living, making it a desirable place to live and visit.

Scenic Views of Hockessin

History

Hockessin's history dates back to the early 18th century. The town was originally settled by Quakers and has grown over the centuries into a vibrant community while maintaining its historic charm. The name "Hockessin" is believed to derive from a Native American word meaning "pieces of bark."

Things to Do

  • Explore the local parks and trails
  • Enjoy the annual Fourth of July parade
  • Discover local shops and restaurants
